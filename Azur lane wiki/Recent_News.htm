January 23rd 2025

New Contents

  1. Project Identity Update:
    • Project Identity 2.0: Oceana added (Unlocked after reaching Commander level 30)
    • Oceana will grow up over a period of 20 weeks. You can set her weekly schedule and also go on outings with her.
    • Each activity has different stat and ability requirements. Some cost money, too. Try to plan your activities around your growth goals. You can spend action points to go outside and visit all kinds of different locations.
    • During Oceana’s growth, special activities will be triggered from time to time, and through them you can unlock corresponding stories and CGs.
    • After each iteration of Oceana’s growth, depending on her stats, personality, and abilities, different endings can be unlocked.
    • After you complete “Project Identity: Oceana” main story chapter “Project Identity: A New Face”, the special secretary function will be unlocked and you can choose Oceana as your special secretary on the main page. By collecting more Moments and Endings, you can unlock more Modules that change her appearance.
